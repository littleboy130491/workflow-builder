# Step 03: Core Pages Identification — Instructions

## Objective

Identify which pages are most critical for the business based on the sitemap structure.

## Process

1. Read the parsed sitemap from `outputs/02-sitemap/sitemap-parsed.md`

2. Analyze the sitemap to identify core business pages. Consider:
   - **Homepage** — Always core
   - **About/About Us** — Brand identity and trust
   - **Contact/Contact Us** — Conversion-critical
   - **Products/Services** — Revenue-driving
   - **Pricing** — If applicable, high-intent
   - **Blog/Resources** — SEO and authority
   - **Testimonials/Case Studies** — Social proof

3. Prioritize pages using this framework:
   - **P0 (Critical)**: Pages directly tied to revenue/conversion
   - **P1 (Important)**: Pages supporting trust and decision-making
   - **P2 (Nice to have)**: Content and SEO pages

4. Save output to `outputs/03-core-pages/core-pages.md` with:
   - Page name
   - URL
   - Priority tier (P0/P1/P2)
   - Justification for why it's core

## Notes

- Limit P0 to 3-5 pages maximum for focus
- P1 should not exceed 5-7 pages
- Document reasoning — future steps depend on understanding why these matter
