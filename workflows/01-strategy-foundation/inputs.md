# Inputs

- Required: business overview and service list
  - Source: user-provided or client-provided materials
  - Use: define the company's offer, scope, and commercial context
  - Ask the user if this information is not already available in repository materials

- Optional: brand materials, pitch deck, or existing website
  - Source: user-provided, client-provided, or repository materials
  - Use: identify current brand signals, messaging patterns, and factual inputs that may shape the strategy brief

- Required: commercial goals, market context, and known competitors
  - Source: user-provided, client-provided, or repository materials
  - Use: clarify what the website needs to achieve and what competitive frame it must respond to
  - Ask the user if these details cannot be derived from available materials

- Optional: existing audience, sales, or positioning notes
  - Source: user-provided, client-provided, or repository materials
  - Use: support faster definition of audience priorities and positioning assumptions
