# 01 Strategy Foundation

## Brief Description

Define the strategic baseline for the company profile website so later content, design, and development decisions are anchored to business reality instead of aesthetics alone.

## Expected Input

- Source materials defined in `/home/henry/Desktop/projects/web-designer/workflows/01-strategy-foundation/inputs.md`

## Expected Outputs

Create the following files inside `/home/henry/Desktop/projects/web-designer/outputs/01-strategy-foundation/`:

- `strategy-brief.md`: Business goals, target outcomes, target audiences, market context, positioning ambition, and site scope
- `assumptions-and-open-questions.md`: Explicit assumptions, unknowns, and any blockers that need resolution

## Success Criteria

- The business objective for the site is clear and prioritized
- Primary conversion goals and trust-building goals are identified
- Core audience and decision context are stated clearly enough to guide messaging
- Site scope is defined tightly enough to prevent unnecessary pages or features
- Assumptions are called out explicitly instead of being hidden inside recommendations

## Constraints

- Do not invent core company facts
- Mark missing information explicitly
- Keep the brief decision-oriented, not academic
- Do not modify files outside `/home/henry/Desktop/projects/web-designer/outputs/01-strategy-foundation/`

## Execution Notes

- Translate vague requests such as "professional" or "outstanding" into concrete site qualities
- Define what success means for this specific company profile site, not for websites in general
- Keep the output concise enough that later steps can use it as an operational brief

## Stop Conditions Or Approval Gates

- Stop and ask the user if required business inputs are missing and cannot be derived from repository materials
- Stop and ask the user if there are multiple plausible business objectives and no evidence for prioritizing one
- Stop and ask the user if the only way to proceed would be to invent unsupported company facts or market claims
